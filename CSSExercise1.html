Persimmon (gam)

Also known as ‘the Korean mango’, there are two main types. The astringent persimmon reflects the mango shape, but the non-astringent persimmon is actually shaped more like a tomato. Their outside skin ranges from pale yellow, to red-orange, with yellow-orange flesh inside with a soft jelly-like texture. Korean fruit name: Persimmon (gam) And they taste sweet; many people compare the flavour to honey. Dried persimmon is useful for drinks such as Korean cinnamon punch, known as ‘sujeonggwa’, as well as for teas..
